# **App Name**: Asnap

## Core Features:

- Secure User Authentication: Allows users to register, log in, and securely manage their accounts to access personalized content.
- Ephemeral Post Creation: Users can upload images or videos with captions, which are automatically deleted 48 hours after posting.
- Personalized Content Feed: Displays an infinite scrollable feed of posts from followed users, reflecting the ephemeral nature of content.
- Dynamic User Profiles: Users can view their own active posts, manage followers, and see who they follow, showcasing their temporary contributions.
- Interactive Story Sharing: Enable users to share short-lived photo or video stories that disappear after 24 hours.
- AI Caption & Hashtag Tool: An AI-powered tool that suggests engaging captions or relevant hashtags for new posts, enhancing user content creation.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Muted Violet (#A67ACE). Chosen for a modern, digital feel, suggesting creativity and connection without being overtly bright. This will be used for interactive elements like buttons and active states.
- Background color: Faded Lavender (#F8F3F9). A very light, almost white background with a subtle hint of the primary hue, providing a clean and fresh canvas appropriate for a light color scheme and ephemeral content.
- Accent color: Vibrant Royal Blue (#382BE0). This contrasting, yet analogous, bold blue will draw attention to key calls to action and important notifications, adding dynamic pops of color.
- Headline and Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif). A modern, clean, and highly readable font that maintains a consistent, objective feel suitable for social media content across all text elements.
- Modern and minimalist outlined icons, emphasizing clarity and quick recognition for actions like liking, commenting, sharing, and content expiry indicators.
- A mobile-first, single-column feed with a spacious design that highlights image and video content. Key navigation elements like home, post creation, and profile will be accessible via a persistent bottom bar. Stories will appear in a horizontally scrollable bar at the top.
- Subtle, smooth transitions for screen navigation and micro-interactions for actions such as liking posts (e.g., a heart icon pop-out). A gentle visual cue or countdown will indicate when posts are nearing their 48-hour expiration.
